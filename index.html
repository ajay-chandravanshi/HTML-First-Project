Veg Chow Mein

Veg Chow Mein

About The Dish

This easy Chinese-Inspired Chow Mein recipe is made with noodles, vegetables and a simple chow mein sauce.It's quick to make, better than Panda
Express chow mein, and healthy too!

  • Cook Time:20-25 min
  • Serves: 4-5 servings

Ingredients of Vegetable Chowmein

INGREDIENTS QUANTITY
fresh noodles 200 gram
water 5 cups
Salt 1 teaspoons
oil 2 teaspoons
Ginger garlic paste 1 teaspoons
Chilli powder 1 teaspoons
onions(sliced) 1/4 cup (2 peice)
Soy sauce 1/2 teaspoons
Celery ,chopped 1/4 cup
Vinegar 1 teaspoons
Chilli Sauce 1 teaspoons
Green and Red Capsicum 1 cup
Mushroom 1 cup
Carrot (shredded) 1 cup
Green Chillies(chopped) 1
Tomato Sauce 1 teaspoons
Spring Onion 1 teaspoons
Garlic(chopped) 1 teaspoons
Black pepper powder 1/2 teaspoons

Chow Mein Sauce
INGREDIENTS QUANTITY
low-sodium soy sauce 1/4 cup
sesame oil 1 1/2 teaspoons
oyster sauce 1 Tablespoon
freshly grated ginger 2 teaspoons
light brown sugar 1 Tablespoon
cornstarch 1 teaspoon

Instructions:

1.Take water in a pan, add salt followed by olive oil and bring to boil.


2.noodles and cook 'bite like' -- just blanch if fresh and cook longer if dry


3.Drain immediately and cool under running water till noodles are thoroughly cooled.


4.Work 1 tablespoon of the oil into the noodles and leave in the colander till required


5.Keep aside a small bowl of green chilli dipped in vinegar for garnishing


6.Now, heat some oil in a heavy bottom pan and stir- fry garlic, ginger garlic paste and onions
over high heat till onions are golden brown.


7.Add celery and mushrooms followed by red and green capsicum along with carrot. Stir well.


8.Now add salt, black pepper powder, tomato sauce, chilli sauce, soy sauce and vinegar to
veggie mixture.Mix well


9.Add the noodles to this and stir well till it is thoroughly mixed.


10.Garnish with red capsicum and pour the prepared green-chilli vinegar mix on top.



11.Serve hot.

Banana Kheer

Banana Kheer

About The Dish

Banana Kheer is a delightful and creamy dessert that is popular in Indian cuisine. Made with ripe bananas, milk, and flavored with aromatic spices, this sweet dish is perfect for special occasions or as a comforting treat. In this recipe, we will guide you through the step-by-step process of making a delicious Banana Kheer that will satisfy your sweet cravings and leave you wanting more.

  • Cook Time:20-25 min
  • Serves: 3-4 servings

Ingredients of Banana Kheer

INGREDIENTS QUANTITY
Bananas 4
milk 4 cups
condensed milk 1/2 cup
sugar (adjust according to taste) 1/4 cup
cardamom powder 1/4 teaspoon
ghee (clarified butter) 1 tablespoon
roughly chopped nuts 1/2 cup
strands kesar 2 cup

Step-by-Step Instructions:

1.Preparing the Bananas:

(a).Mash Banana using a fork or potato masher until smooth. Set them aside.

2.Roasting the Nuts:

(a).Heat the ghee in a small pan over medium heat.

(b).Add the chopped nuts and sauté until they turn golden brown.

(c).Remove the roasted nuts from the pan and set them aside for garnishing later.

3.Preparing the Kheer Base:

(a).In a large heavy-bottomed saucepan, pour the milk and bring it to a boil over medium heat.

(b).Once the milk starts boiling, reduce the heat to low and let it simmer, stirring occasionally to prevent the milk from sticking to the bottom.

4.Adding the Sweetness:

(a).Add the condensed milk to the simmering milk and mix well

(b).Next, add the sugar and cardamom powder, and stir until the sugar dissolves completely.

(c).Continue to simmer the milk for about 10 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

5.Incorporating the Bananas:

(a).Add the mashed bananas to the simmering milk mixture and mix well.

(b).Cook the kheer on low heat for another 5 minutes, stirring continuously to prevent lumps from forming

6.Enhancing the Flavor:

(a).If desired, crush a few saffron strands between your fingers and add them to the kheer for a hint of aroma and color. Stir well.

7.Garnishing and Serving:

(a).Once the kheer reaches a desired consistency, turn off the heat and let it cool slightly.

(b).Transfer the kheer to serving bowls or glasses.

(c).Garnish with the roasted nuts .

(d).Serve the Banana Kheer warm or chilled, according to your preference.
